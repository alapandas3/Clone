Redway Networks is an IT infrastructure reseller who provides high-performance cloud-networking solutions that deliver the security and control businesses, organisations and education establishments need to support digital transformation. We use our proven expertise in WiFi, networking, switching, firewalls and security solutions to provide our customers with future-proofed, scalable networks that support growth and innovation.

WiFi Survey

A WiFi survey plays a crucial part in designing and maintaining high-performance wireless networks. All our surveys are conducted by our certified network engineers using Ekahau meaning we can design networks that deliver 100% coverage.

Wireless Solutions

In todays hybrid environment you need WiFi that works harder for your business. Our reliable, secure wireless networks deliver the high-performance connectivity, capacity and coverage you need to support digital transformation and power user experiences.

Network Solutions
